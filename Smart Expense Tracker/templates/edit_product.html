About Products

Products represent individual items or expenses that are part of a bill.

How to edit a product:

  1. Update the product name if needed
  2. Adjust the price if necessary
  3. Modify which members shared this expense

Expense Splitting:

The cost of each product will be split equally among all selected members. For example, if a $30 product is shared by 3 members, each member will owe $10.
